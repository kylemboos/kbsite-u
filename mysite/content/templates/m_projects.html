Augmented Reality w/ Depth

interesting...

By using the depth sensor provided by the Kinect, 2d and 3d overlays can be displayed on the environment such as walls and tables. Extended applications involve sharing a created 3d model between multiple users.

Android Vehicle

interesting...

The autonomous android vehicle (AAV) utilizes openCV and the ioio circuit board with a modified RC car to track and follow objects of a specific size and color. This project later led to the establishment of RoboEaters, the official RoboCup Search and Rescue team for UCI.
